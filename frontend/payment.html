What you get

Cleaner Kit

Cleaner Kit helps you quickly find and remove duplicate photos, large videos, and unnecessary calendar events. It also detects and deletes duplicate contacts, helping to keep your address book clean and organized. You can even remove GPS metadata from your photos to protect your privacy. All-in-one cleanup to keep your device running smoothly.

Smart Compress

Smart Compress reduces the size of your photos and videos without compromising quality. This feature helps free up valuable storage space while keeping your memories intact. Ideal for users with limited space who want a lighter, faster device.

Sort Photos

Sort Photos makes cleaning up your gallery simple and intuitive. Just swipe left or right to decide which photos to keep and which to delete. It’s a fast and fun way to clear space without missing anything important.

Speed Test
